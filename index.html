Welcome, Fapstronaut!

Are you tired of all those feature impoverished day counters that do nothing more than, well, count days? Then look no further! Our completely open source, multi platform NofApp App aims to complete this gap. Tracking your streaks of abstinence, your latest encounters under the sheets and even your psyche, although subjective (bluetooth brain implant will be kickstarted around 2020) is possible and encouraged.

On Google Play

NofApp is completely free to download on the Google Play Store and gets updated as we devise and implement new features. Get your copy NOW.

(Not yet) In the iTunes Appstore

At some point in the future we will release this glorious app in the iTunes Appstore. Although the conditions are quite tough, we will do our best to get there, as soon as we have a developer account.

But wait, there's more!

The following is a list (not in order) of features we want to implement in the near future. Help us reach our goals by forking us on GitHub and contributing to the app. You will earn our and all fapstronaut's eternal gratitude.

  • Account based data to save and restore your progress
  • Export function to use your data however you please
  • MOAR graphs and analytics on your history of pleasure
