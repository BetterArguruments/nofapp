Explanation of Parameters

NofApp helps you track your well-being while abstaining from Fapping.

Here's a quick explanation of the different parameters.

Mood

How you are feeling, e.g. depressed, happy, euphoric.

Energy

Your motivation to do (new) things. Low Energy would be just lying around in bed and surfing on Facebook, whereas High Energy would be working / being productive / going out and meeting new people.

Libido

Well, this is pretty self-explanatory. The probability of you humping the tree beside you.

Last Sex

The last time you had sex (no, not with your hand).

Last Fap

The last time you fapped. Be honest.
