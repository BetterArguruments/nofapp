The Fapsperiment is all about proving that Nofap works. To accomplish that, you can provide us with anonymous data of your Nofap progress.

Data about your Mood, Energy, Libido, Sex and Faps is submitted under an anonymous User ID. Your Notes are personal and will not be submitted.

If you have concerns regarding your privacy, fear no more: NofApp and NofApp.com are completely open-source (see Settings - About for GitHub Links). Furthermore, the collected data can be accessed (and analyzed) by anyone as it is hosted publicly on Firebase.
