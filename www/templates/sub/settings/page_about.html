About NofAPP
Version 0.0.2 "Ejaculatio Praecox"

Once upon a time, two highly motivated German Students, who happened to be friends, met up during their University Holidays for a private "Hackathon" to utilize their 1337 Programm0r Skillz.

They decided to develop an app. But what type of app? One student had a medical background, the other was studying mechanical engineering.

What a perfect combination! They wondered whether Nofap could actually prevent depression and anxiety (therefore the questions about mood and energy) and if one actually has more sex after beginning Nofap.

However, after about 30 seconds they noticed, that only by using a framework they would stand a realistic chance of creating an app with more than one page in 48 hours.

They finally chose the Ionic Framework, which turned out to be awesome (thanks, Ionic Framework peoples!).

Enjoy and keep No-Fapping!
